Tonic Rolls Out New Control Features...and Dark Mode!

Tonic, the new discovery app that gives you a personalized selection of reads every day, has just been updated to give you more control.

We’re always looking for ways to give you more control over the content you are seeing each day so we just shipped a feature that allows you to swap out up to five daily picks if you feel they aren’t right for you. Our algorithm game is strong and our editors are brilliant but we realize that sometimes we might not get it right, so we wanted to give you an opportunity to find something different each day and change your picks. Simply long press the recommendation that you want to swap.

The new version of Tonic also has an updated the on-boarding flow so that new users will get fresh recommendations on day one. We’re also rolling out a new layout that updates the daily picks and recent bookmarks sections to make the reading experience silky smooth. 

Last but not least, we’re pleased to announce that you can now enjoy Tonic in dark mode.

‍‍Tonic is powered by Canopy’s private discovery architecture and by human curators, so you are recommended the best of the internet while your data stays secure.

Tonic is available on iOS from the Apple App Store. Let us know what you think by sending us a note at hello@canopy.cr or find us on Twitter at @tonic_app.
